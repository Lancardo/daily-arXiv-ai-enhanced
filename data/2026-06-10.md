### [1] [Effective Training Principles of Physical Reservoirs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10130)
*Sobhi Saeed,Mehmet Müftüoglu,Glitta R. Cheeran,Juliane Heim,Bennet Fischer,Mario Chemnitz*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 本文研究了光储层计算中通过输出剪枝和正则化来缓解过拟合与降低计算开销的策略，比较了多种方法，并在非线性光纤极限学习机上进行了验证。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 光储层计算虽利用光学复杂性提供丰富非线性动力学，但直接训练输出层易导致过拟合且计算效率低下，因此需要有效的输出处理策略。

Method: 比较了四种输出剪枝方法（Equal Search、Branch and Bound、Variance Filter、随机剪枝）以及L1/L2正则化技术（LASSO和岭回归），并探究了跨全输出频谱选择读出权重对性能的影响。

Result: 跨全输出频谱选择读出权重可提升非迭代方法的性能；L1和L2正则化在高度非线性任务（如Spiral Benchmark）上显著提升效果；不同剪枝方法各有优劣，其中信息驱动的剪枝策略优于随机剪枝。

Conclusion: 本研究深入分析了储层隐藏层滤波机制与输出层训练方法，为物理储层计算系统实现优化性能提供了指导。

Abstract: Reservoir computers benefit from the inherent complexity of optical phenomena, which provide rich, often nonlinear dynamics. However, training directly on the reservoir's output renders the system prone to overfitting and computationally inefficient during the training phase. In this work, we investigate strategies to mitigate overfitting and reduce computational overhead through output pruning and regularization. We compare loss-minimizing search methods (Equal Search and Branch and Bound) against an output-oriented statistical filtering approach (Variance Filter) and random pruning, highlighting advantages and disadvantages of each approach and the overall importance of informed reservoir output sampling, particularly for a shrinking latent space. We further demonstrate that enforcing readout selection across the full output spectrum improves performance, especially for non-iterative methods. Additionally, we examine L1 and L2 regularization techniques (LASSO and ridge regression), both of which significantly enhance performance on highly nonlinear tasks such as the Spiral Benchmark. While our methods are of general use, results are obtained from and discussed exemplarily for a nonlinear fiber-optical extreme learning machine. Overall, this study provides a deep analysis of the reservoirs' hidden-layer filtering mechanisms and the output-layer training, enabling optimized performance in physical reservoir computing systems.

Main category: physics.optics

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 光学神经网络的天然优势在于空间复用，但以往主要将其用于并行吞吐。本文试图挖掘空间复用的更深层能力——将其定义为模型的可训练表征坐标，而不仅仅是数据处理通道。

Method: 采用可编程自由空间光学处理器，通过在线物理前向/替代反向（physical-forward/surrogate-backward）方案训练：前向由实际光学测量输出定义，反向通过可微替代模型估计梯度，并在训练过程中利用新采集的光学数据持续微调替代模型。

Result: 在三个场景（并行输入处理、类编码读出、通道混合特征交互）中实现了空间通道的不同角色，完成了图像分类与回归任务，并成功构建了混合光电视觉-语言模型，光学神经网络为数字Transformer解码器提供视觉令牌以完成可控图像描述。

Conclusion: 空间复用光学通道可被确立为混合光电视觉模型的可编程特征与读出空间，拓展了光学神经网络在表征学习中的潜力。

Abstract: Spatial multiplexing is one of the natural strengths of optics, yet in optical neural networks, it is often used mainly as parallel throughput. Here, we show that spatial multiplexing in an optical neural network can be used not only to process multiple inputs in parallel, but also to define a trainable representational coordinate of the model. In three implemented scenarios, parallel-input processing, class-code readout and channel-mixed feature interaction, spatial channels act as independent learners, structured code dimensions, and interacting feature groups. The programmable free-space optical processor is trained through an online physical-forward/surrogate-backward scheme, where measured optical outputs define the forward pass while a differentiable surrogate estimates gradients and is continually fine-tuned during training from newly acquired optical data. We demonstrate these channel roles in image classification and regression tasks using multi-layer architectures with more than one million trainable optical phase parameters. We further implement a hybrid optical-electronic vision-language model, in which the optical neural network provides visual tokens to a digital transformer decoder for controlled image-captioning tasks. These results establish spatially multiplexed optical channels as a programmable feature and readout space for hybrid optical vision models.

### [3] [Unidirectional-like Edge Transport Induced by Non-Hermitian Skin Effects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10291)
*Junyi Rao,Jinting Ding,Qinghua Guo,Jun He,Xiang Ni*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 本文通过理论和实验证明，在具有相同陈数但不同体极化的光子晶体中，均匀损耗可以激活非厄米趋肤效应，将非手性边界态转变为单向传播，为光波单向传输提供了简单通用的新机制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 非厄米趋肤效应通常需要工程化的非互易性或空间图案化损耗来实现，实验实现复杂。本文探索均匀损耗这一被忽视的简单机制是否能实现类单向边界传输。

Method: 采用芯-包层几何结构，两个区域陈数相同但体极化不同，在均匀损耗条件下，利用非厄米趋肤效应重塑边缘带的谱拓扑，产生点隙缠绕，驱动单向传播。通过近场测量进行实验验证。

Result: 均匀损耗成功将原本双向传播的界面态转变为围绕整个畴壁的单向循环，理论与实验高度吻合。

Conclusion: 均匀损耗是实现类单向波传输的一种通用且结构简单的途径，为光波调控提供了新范式。

Abstract: Non-Hermitian skin effects (NHSEs) enable dramatic boundary accumulation of waves, yet their experimental realization typically demands engineered nonreciprocity or spatially patterned loss. Here we demonstrate theoretically and experimentally that uniform loss provides a simple and previously overlooked mechanism for enforcing unidirectional-like edge transport in photonic crystals (PhCs) that breaks time-reversal symmetry in the presence of nonchiral edge states. Using a core cladding geometry where domains share identical Chern numbers but possess distinct bulk polarizations, we show that uniform loss activates NHSEs that reshape the spectral topology of edge bands, giving rise to point gap windings that dictate a one way propagation. Near field measurements confirm that loss converts intrinsically bidirectional interface states into a unidirectional-like circulation around the entire domain wall, showing excellent agreement with theory. Our results establish uniform loss as a universal and structurally simple route for achieving unidirectional-like wave transport.

### [4] [Intrinsic plasmon canalization in the biaxial van der Waals crystal MoOCl$_2$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10534)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 利用范德华晶体MoOCl₂中的Drude介电常数跨越零点的特性，在室温下通过近场光学成像直接观测等离激元-极化激元的通道化传播。通过调节材料厚度，实验和理论上证明了通道化波长可在超过1 μm范围内调谐。

Abstract: Anisotropic polaritons in low-symmetry crystals allow for subwavelength confinement and directional routing of light. The most extreme form of such anisotropy arises at the topological transition between elliptical and hyperbolic dispersion, where the isofrequency contours collapse into parallel lines and polaritons propagate in a diffractionless, beam-like fashion. This canalization regime has previously been accessed through twisted heterostructures or engineered metasurfaces. Here we show that natural canalization can be achieved without any fabrication or structuring by exploiting the intrinsic elliptical-to-hyperbolic transition in the van der Waals crystal MoOCl$_2$ at room temperature. Using near-field imaging, we directly visualize plasmon-polariton canalization emerging at the low-loss Drude crossing point along the [010] crystal axis. Owing to the moderate slope of the Drude permittivity, the resulting polaritons remain highly directional across a broad spectral window. This weak dispersion also enables robust thickness-dependent tuning, and we demonstrate, both experimentally and theoretically, that the canalization wavelength can be adjusted by more than 1 μm simply by varying the flake thickness. This work brings canalized polariton propagation into the 4.5 - 6 μm range, beyond the frequency limits of phonon-polariton platforms and overlapping with important molecular vibrations, opening new opportunities for mid-IR nanophotonics and sensing.
