### [1] [Photonic Neuromorphic Computing enabled by a BIC Metasurface](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22528)
*Jingsong Fu,Ruiheng Jin,Zhaohui Xie,Haijun Tang,Xiong Jiang,Yue Cui,Xiangtong Kong,Wentao Hao,Geyang Qu,Can Huang,Qingha Song*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 提出基于BIC主动超表面的单片光子循环网络，实现非线性、密集连接和固有记忆的协同，构建片上物理储层计算机

Motivation: 光子神经形态计算面临的关键挑战：如何单片集成非线性、密集连接和固有记忆，以实现脑启发的时空信息处理

Method: 利用BIC模式在晶格中实现强长程耦合，构建可重构循环网络拓扑；增益介质提供光学非线性（神经元激活）和有限载流子寿命（模拟时间记忆）

Result: 在脑MRI图像分类和人类动作识别基准任务上分别达到92.16%和85.36%的准确率

Abstract: Photonic neuromorphic computing promises revolutionary advances in parallel and high-speed processing, yet a key challenge persists: co-integrating nonlinearity, dense connectivity, and intrinsic memory monolithically to enable brain-inspired, spatiotemporal information processing. Here, we overcome this challenge by introducing a monolithic photonic recurrent network based on an active metasurface operating at bound state in the continuum (BIC). The BIC mode mediates strong,long-range coupling across the lattice, creating a reconfigurable recurrent network topology in hardware. Concurrently, the gain medium provides both optical nonlinearity for neuronal activation and a finite carrier lifetime that serves as a built in, analog temporal memory. This synergy enables computation to emerge directly from the collective spatiotemporal dynamics of the driven-dissipative photonic system, effectively realizing a physical reservoir computer on a chip. We experimentally validate a minimal yet physically complete system on benchmark tasks: brain MRI image classification and human action recognition, achieving 92.16% and 85.36% accuracies, respectively. This work establishes a scalable pathway toward ultrafast, energy-efficient neuromorphic intelligence where processing is an inherent property of tailored light matter interaction.

### [2] [Anomalous High-Energy Second Plateau in High Harmonic Generation from Fullerenes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22599)
*Km Akanksha Dubey,Ofer Neufeld*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 研究发现富勒烯高次谐波产生存在异常的第二平台，其截止能量远超经典预期，并提出了一种新的量子共振机制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索复杂结构量子系统中的高次谐波产生机制，特别是富勒烯家族中观察到的异常第二平台现象，以寻找无需中红外驱动即可产生高能相干宽带辐射的新途径。

Method: 采用从头算理论方法，系统研究从C20到C60（包括异构体）的气相富勒烯在不同激光条件（不同椭圆度、角度取向、强度和波长）下的高次谐波产生。

Result: 发现富勒烯高次谐波产生存在显著的高能第二平台，其截止能量远超经典预期（如800nm激光下可达115eV），该机制与标准SFA机制不同，具有反常的波长依赖性（截止能量随波长增加而减小直至饱和）和线性电场幅度依赖性。

Conclusion: 富勒烯中的异常第二平台机制源于量子力学共振效应，而非实空间轨迹图像，这为无需中红外驱动即可实现高能相干宽带辐射开辟了新途径，揭示了复杂结构量子系统中新颖的高次谐波产生机制。

Abstract: We explore with ab-initio theory high harmonic generation (HHG) from a series of gas-phase fullerenes (from C$_{20}$ to C$_{60}$, including isomers) under varying laser conditions (different ellipticities, angular orientations, intensities and wavelengths). We find that HHG emission from fullerenes exhibits a prominent high-energy second plateau, extending well above the expected semi-classical cutoff, e.g. for an 800 nm driving laser with a peak intensity of $10^{14}$ W/cm$^2$, the anomalous $2^{nd}$ plateau cutoff is 115 eV. We theoretically analyze the underlying $2^{nd}$ plateau physical mechanism and determine that: (i) It differs from the standard SFA-like mechanism; (ii) It is recombination- based; (iii) It has an unusual inverted cutoff scaling with wavelength (whereby the cutoff decreases with increasing wavelength, until reaching a saturation); and (iv) It exhibits a linear cutoff dependence with electric field amplitude E$_0$ . We further rule out real-space trajectory based pictures, indicating that the most likely culprits are sharp quantum mechanical resonances in the fullerene family. Our work establishes a new route for high energy coherent broadband emission without requiring mid-IR driving and uncovers a novel HHG mechanism in complex structure quantum systems.

### [3] [Emerging Multidimensional Real-Space Topological Structures at Chiral Bound States in the Continuum](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22634)
*Xingqi Zhao,Jingguang Chen,Jiajun Wang,Lixi Rao,Wenzhe Liu,Fang Guan,C. T. Chan,Lei Shi,Jian Zi*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 该研究揭示了磁性诱导的手性连续谱束缚态具有扩展到实空间的多维拓扑结构，并通过实验在旋磁光子晶体中实现了手性BIC，展示了其丰富的拓扑特性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 连续谱束缚态作为广泛研究的拓扑奇点，其动量空间拓扑已展现出丰富的物理特性。然而，其拓扑结构是否可扩展到实空间维度尚待探索。本研究旨在揭示磁性诱导的手性BIC在实空间中的多维拓扑结构。

Method: 设计并实现了旋磁光子晶体板，通过磁场打破时间反演对称性，解除BIC的简并，产生具有相反圆偏振的手性BIC对。采用近场扫描测量技术，观察相位涡旋、拓扑荷、空间分布的手性近场以及磁控产生的斯格明子斯托克斯纹理。

Result: 实验成功观测到：1）具有量子化拓扑荷的相位涡旋；2）空间分布的手性近场；3）磁控产生的斯格明子斯托克斯纹理。这些结果表明手性BIC在实空间中展现出丰富的多维拓扑结构。

Conclusion: 该工作揭示了BIC拓扑中先前未被探索的实空间维度，建立了旋磁光子晶体作为操纵复杂拓扑态的多功能平台，为拓扑光子学开辟了新的研究方向。

Abstract: As widely studied topological singularities, bound states in the continuum (BICs) have revealed rich physical properties through their momentum-space topology. Here, we reveal and experimentally demonstrate that magnetically induced chiral BICs possess multidimensional topological structures extending into real space. We design and realize a gyromagnetic photonic crystal slab where magnetic field breaks the time-reversal symmetry and lifts the degeneracy of BICs, creating a pair of chiral BICs with opposite circular polarizations. Near-field scanning measurements reveal phase vortices with quantized topological charges, spatially distributed near-field chirality, and skyrmionic Stokes textures arising from magnetic control. Our work unveils a previously unexplored dimension of BIC topology and establishes gyromagnetic photonic crystals as versatile platforms for manipulating complex topological states.

### [4] [Quantum squeezing in an all-resonant periodically poled lithium niobate microresonator](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22693)
*Xinyi Ren,Reshma Kopparapu,Tushar Sanjay Karnik,Chun-Ho Lee,Kiwon Kwon,Clayton Cheung,Yue Yu,Shi-Yuan Ma,Bo-Han Wu,Ran Yin,Lian Zhou,Quntao Zhuang,Dirk Englund,Zaijun Chen,Mengjie Yu*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 该论文在薄膜铌酸锂芯片上实现了基于χ(2)非线性效应的强宽带压缩光产生，创造了集成χ(2)腔平台中最高的压缩比，为量子增强传感和计量提供了可扩展的功率高效压缩光源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统基于χ(3)非线性的片上压缩光源受限于泵浦功率和额外损耗，而χ(2)平台具有更强的参量相互作用、更低的泵浦功率要求和更大的光谱工程灵活性，但片上实现面临挑战。

Method: 采用单周期极化铌酸锂微谐振器实现双共振光学参量放大器，在X切薄膜PPLN谐振器中实现近全深度畴反转和高度过耦合共振，在连续波状态下以793.5nm泵浦产生1587nm压缩光。

Result: 直接测量到-0.81dB的压缩（低于散粒噪声极限）和+4.29dB的反压缩，推断片上压缩水平为-7.52dB±0.22dB，反压缩为+9.62dB±0.25dB，单模压缩光谱超过10.3THz，创造了集成χ(2)腔平台最高压缩比。

Conclusion: 该工作首次实现了准相位匹配、全共振的片上χ(2)腔压缩器，为完全集成功率高效的压缩光源提供了可扩展路径，将推动量子增强传感和计量学的发展。

Abstract: Quantum noise limits the sensitivity of optical measurements, but squeezed states of light enable quantum-enhanced metrology, sensing, and information processing. Most on-chip squeezed-light sources rely on Kerr ($χ^{(3)}$) nonlinearities, remain limited by pump power and excess loss constraints. Quadratic ($χ^{(2)}$) platforms instead provide stronger parametric interactions, lower pump power requirements, and greater spectral engineering flexibility. Here, we demonstrate strong, broadband squeezed-light generation on a thin-film lithium niobate (TFLN) photonic chip using a dual-resonant optical parametric amplifier implemented in a single periodically poled LN (PPLN) microresonator. Near-full-depth domain inversion is achieved simultaneously with highly over-coupled resonances, exhibiting escape efficiencies exceeding 90% and intrinsic quality factors above 2.5 million in a 0.6 mm$^2$ X-cut TF-PPLN resonator, enabling efficient squeezing at 1587 nm when pumped at 793.5 nm. Operating in the continuous-wave regime, we directly measure -0.81 dB of squeezing below the shot-noise limit with a pump power of 27 mW, together with +4.29 dB of anti-squeezing. From these measurements, we infer an on-chip squeezing level of -7.52 dB $\pm$ 0.22 dB (95% confidence interval: [-7.96,-7.10] dB), and an on-chip anti-squeezing level of +9.62 dB $\pm$ 0.25 dB. We demonstrate single-mode squeezing at degeneracy with a squeezed-light spectrum exceeding 10.3 THz. This work reports the highest squeezing ratio among integrated $χ^{(2)}$ cavity platforms and the first quasi-phase matched, fully resonant $χ^{(2)}$ cavity squeezer on chip, establishing a scalable route to fully integrated power-efficient squeezed-light sources for quantum-enhanced sensing and metrology.
