Method: 在光学光纤腔内通过同时进行相位和幅度调制，在频域中实现非互易光传播，将两个Hatano-Nelson链编码在电光频率梳的谐振模式中。

  <summary>Details</summary>

This provides a consistent, metric-based elucidation for the intrinsic limitations of concentration in passive metamaterials and extreme-index platforms.
