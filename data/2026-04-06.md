Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种拓扑安德森随机激光器（TARL），将拓扑激光和随机激光两种对立范式统一起来，利用无序本身诱导拓扑相变，实现鲁棒单模激光。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 拓扑激光通过受保护的边缘输运抑制无序，而随机激光则利用多重散射提供反馈。这两种看似不相容的范式能否统一？该研究旨在探索通过无序本身诱导拓扑相变，实现兼具两者优点的激光器。

Method: 从平凡光子晶格出发，通过工程化无序驱动系统进入拓扑安德森绝缘体相，产生涌现的手性边缘态作为边界选择性激光通道。理论分析无序强度与拓扑迁移率能隙的关系。

Result: TARL表现出向单一边缘态的快速模式选择，产生超窄发射光谱和增强的斜率效率，在无序强度接近最大拓扑迁移率能隙时达到最优。具有类单模相干特性，偏离传统手性拓扑激光的Kardar-Parisi-Zhang行为，同时比传统随机激光对局部扰动更鲁棒。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了一种无序启发的灵活途径来实现拓扑保护的单模激光，为鲁棒、高相干光子光源引入了全新的设计原理，统一了拓扑激光和随机激光两种对立范式。

Abstract: Topological lasers and random lasers embody two contrasting strategies for disorder management in photonics: the former suppresses disorder via protected edge transport, while the latter exploits multiple scattering for feedback. Here, we theoretically demonstrate that these seemingly incompatible paradigms can be unified through a topological Anderson random laser (TARL), where disorder itself induces a topological phase that enables robust lasing. Starting from a trivial photonic lattice, we show that engineered disorder drives the system into a topological Anderson insulator regime, generating emergent chiral edge states that serve as boundary-selective lasing channels. Remarkably, the TARL exhibits rapid mode selection toward a single edge state, producing an ultranarrow emission spectrum and enhanced slope efficiency optimized near disorder strength with maximal topological mobility gap. Furthermore, they exhibit single-mode-like coherence properties, deviating from Kardar-Parisi-Zhang behavior in conventional chiral topological lasers, while remaining significantly more robust against local perturbations than conventional random lasers. Our findings establish a disorder-enabled flexible route to topologically protected single-mode lasing and introduce a fundamentally new design principle for robust, high-coherence photonic light sources.
