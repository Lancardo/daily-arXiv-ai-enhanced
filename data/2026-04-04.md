### [1] [Quasi-bandgap behavior in non-Hermitian photonic crystals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01319)
*Jin Xu,Daniel Cui,Aaswath P. Raman*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 非厄米光子晶体中，损耗和非损耗材料具有相同实部介电常数但不同虚部，引入少量损耗可在布里渊区边界打开准带隙，产生尖锐反射峰，应用于选择性反射器。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究具有相同实部介电常数但不同虚部（损耗特性）的非厄米光子晶体，探索损耗对光子晶体带隙和反射特性的影响，为设计新型光学器件提供理论基础。

Method: 分析一维和二维非厄米光子晶体的复能带结构和反射响应，使用二阶微扰理论解释准带隙和反射峰的产生机制，设计结合传统光子晶体波导和非厄米光子晶体的选择性反射器。

Result: 即使引入少量材料损耗，也能在布里渊区边界打开准带隙，这在无损耗的相同结构中不存在；准带隙产生尖锐的反射率峰值；成功实现结合传统光子晶体波导和非厄米光子晶体的选择性反射器，实现波长选择性反射和宽带吸收。

Conclusion: 非厄米光子晶体中材料损耗的引入能显著改变光子晶体的光学特性，产生准带隙和尖锐反射峰，为设计新型波长选择性光学器件提供了有效途径，展示了非厄米系统在光子学应用中的潜力。

Abstract: We investigate non-Hermitian photonic crystals in which the lossy and lossless constituents share the same real permittivity and differ only in their imaginary part. We characterize the complex band structure and reflection response of both one-dimensional (1D) and two-dimensional (2D) systems, and show that introducing even a small amount of material loss opens a quasi bandgap at the Brillouin-zone boundary. This quasi bandgap, absent in the lossless limit of the same structure, gives rise to sharp reflectivity peaks whose origin we explain through second-order perturbation theory. As an application of this behavior, we demonstrate a selective reflector combining a conventional photonic-crystal waveguide with a non-Hermitian photonic crystal, achieving wavelength-selective reflection with broadband absorption.

### [2] [Goos-Hänchen Shift in $\mathcal{PT}$-Symmetric and Passive Cavity Optomechanical Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01739)
*Shah Fahad,Gao Xianlong*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 该论文理论上研究了在PT对称和传统光力学系统中弱探测场反射的Goos-Hänchen位移的控制，发现非厄米系统能显著增强位移并实现主动调控。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究PT对称光力学系统中Goos-Hänchen位移的控制，旨在探索非厄米系统中光束位移的增强效应和主动调控能力，为可调谐光子器件和精密光学传感提供新途径。

Method: 采用单光力学平台，其中被动光学腔与主动机械谐振器耦合，分析本征频率谱揭示特殊点的出现，结合传输矩阵法和稳相分析法研究不同PT相中的位移特性。

Result: 横向位移表现出强烈的相位依赖性：在未破缺PT相中相对于破缺相和被动配置显著增强，且可通过腔失谐和腔内介质长度主动调控位移。

Conclusion: 该研究为光力学系统中光束位移的操控提供了可控手段，并指出了实现可调谐光子组件和精密光学传感的潜在途径。

Abstract: We theoretically investigate the control of the Goos-Hänchen shift (GHS) of a reflected weak probe field in both parity-time ($\mathcal{PT}$)-symmetric and conventional optomechanical systems. The proposed scheme consists of a single optomechanical platform where a passive optical cavity is coupled to an active mechanical resonator, in contrast to standard passive-passive configurations. Analysis of the eigenfrequency spectrum reveals the emergence of an exceptional point under balanced gain-loss conditions at a tunable effective optomechanical coupling strength. Using the transfer-matrix method combined with stationary-phase analysis, we examine the GHS across broken and unbroken $\mathcal{PT}$ phases and compare it with that in the conventional system. The lateral shift exhibits strong phase dependence: it is markedly enhanced in the unbroken regime relative to both the broken phase and the passive configuration. We further show that the GHS can be actively tuned through the cavity detuning and the intracavity medium length. These results provide a controlled means for manipulating beam shifts in optomechanical systems and suggest pathways toward tunable photonic components and precision optical sensing.
