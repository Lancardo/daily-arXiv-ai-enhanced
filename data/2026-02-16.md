Method: 采用动态光学相干断层扫描（DOCT）技术，结合两种算法：对数强度方差和晚期OCT相关衰减速度，分别表征动态散射体的占据情况和运动速度。同时整合偏振敏感OCT（PS-OCT）和OCT血管成像（OCTA），并进行组织学验证。

Our results show that skin stripes exhibit high DOCT signals and it varies among the pigment-cell types. Furthermore, the combination of DOCT and PS-OCT captures the maturation of these stripes. ### [3] [The Refractive Index of Gallium Antimonide](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12862)

Our results are based on two complementary measurements on a GaSb/AlAsSb-based heteroepitaxial structure under controlled environmental conditions: photometric transmission and layer-thickness analysis by cross-sectional scanning electron microscopy. We simultaneously retrieve the refractive indices of the two materials by fitting a Sellmeier equation and a theoretical dispersion model by Djurišić \textit{et al.}~\cite{djurisic_modeling_2000}. The uncertainties of $n^*_{\text{GaSb}}$ and $n_{\text{AlAsSb}}$ are quantified using a Monte Carlo-based approach. Our results provide accurate complex refractive index values for GaSb, which are vital to advance photonics-related technologies in the near- and mid infrared spectral region.
