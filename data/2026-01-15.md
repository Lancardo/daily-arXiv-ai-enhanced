Motivation: 研究有限波导阵列中异常点的存在与稳定性，旨在理解非厄米系统中对称性破缺的机制，为设计利用或避免异常点的稳健光学结构提供指导。

Method: 通过解析推导和数值分析相结合的方法，研究有限波导阵列在单点耗散条件下的异常点行为，特别关注阵列波导数量奇偶性对异常点分布的影响。

Result: 发现异常点分布格局由几何相关的奇偶效应决定，偶数与奇数波导阵列表现出严格不同的光谱行为，明确了这些奇点出现和演化的条件。

Conclusion: 阐明了有限非厄米晶格中对称性破缺的机制，为设计利用或避免异常点的稳健光学结构提供了新的指导原则。

Abstract: We explore the existence and stability of exceptional points (EPs) in finite waveguide arrays subject to single-site dissipation. We show that the EP landscape is dictated by a geometry-dependent parity effect, leading to strictly distinct spectral behaviors for arrays with even versus odd numbers of waveguides. Through analytical derivation and numerical analysis, we define the conditions under which these singularities emerge and evolve. Our findings clarify the mechanisms of symmetry breaking in finite non-Hermitian lattices, offering new guidelines for the design of robust optical structures that exploit or avoid exceptional points.
