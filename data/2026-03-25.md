We mapped the lasing cavity mode's near-field, showing that both whispering gallery and Fabry-Perot modes can participate in the lasing. Our results demonstrate how the near-field of a NWL under operation evolves in the sub-picoseconds and the nanometer scales. We anticipate that a direct observation of the near-field will help to elucidate the influence of materials heterogeneities (defects, chemical changes, contaminants, interface roughness, strain) in NWL operation.
