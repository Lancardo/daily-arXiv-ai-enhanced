Motivation: 无透镜相机（如DiffuserCam）使用薄扩散器替代传统镜头，具有紧凑优势，但需要通过计算重建图像，因为扩散器的点扩散函数（PSF）会将每个场景点全局多路复用到传感器上。现有重建方法存在局限性，需要更有效的解决方案。

Method: 提出傅里叶神经算子（FNO）框架用于DiffuserCam重建任务。由于线性移不变前向模型在傅里叶空间中简化为逐点乘法，FNO层的谱域核结构与DiffuserCam逆问题结构对齐。使用紧凑DiffuserCam原型和25,000张自然场景数据集进行训练。

Result: FNO相比参数数量相当的U-Net基线提升2.14dB PSNR和0.11 SSIM。仅在128×128分辨率训练的FNO能够重建256×256和512×512测量值，PSNR损失小于1dB且无需重新训练，展示了分辨率无关的推理能力。

Conclusion: FNO框架为基于薄扩散器的无透镜相机提供了有效的重建解决方案，具有优于传统方法的性能和分辨率无关的推理优势。该框架可直接应用于其他具有全局PSF的无透镜成像模态，如多模光纤内窥镜。

Abstract: Lensless cameras based on thin diffusers offer a compact alternative to conventional refractive imaging but rely on computational reconstruction, since the diffuser's point spread function (PSF) globally multiplexes every scene point across the sensor. Here, we report a Fourier Neural Operator (FNO) framework for this reconstruction task. Because a linear shift-invariant forward model reduces to a pointwise multiplication in Fourier space, the spectral-domain kernel of an FNO layer is structurally aligned with the DiffuserCam inverse problem. Using a compact DiffuserCam prototype and a 25,000-image natural-scene dataset, our FNO improves upon a U-Net baseline of comparable parameter count by $2.14$~dB in PSNR and $0.11$ in SSIM. The same FNO, trained exclusively at $128 \times 128$, reconstructs $256 \times 256$ and $512 \times 512$ measurements with less than $1$~dB loss in PSNR and no retraining, demonstrating resolution-agnostic inference. The framework is directly applicable to other lensless modalities with global PSFs, such as multimode-fiber endoscopy.
